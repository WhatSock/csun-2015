The tooltip is optomized for screen reader and keyboard only users. 

Set focus on one of the active elements,
then press Tab and Shift+Tab to navigate between them.
Tabbing away from an active element with a tooltip, or pressing Escape, will close the tooltip.
Mouse users can mouse over and out of the active element to show or hide the tooltip as usual.
